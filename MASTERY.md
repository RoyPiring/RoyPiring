## 📌 What this is

This is my self-directed side of the house, separate from the [engineering portfolio](./README.md). I took the shape of a PhD, years of deliberate study in one field, and rebuilt it as a run of hands-on end-to-end project builds. Each track aims at up to 100 of them. Together they take an amateur to principal-level command of the field: the point where you could run the practice or be the person others ask.

**What it is.** Deliberate practice in public. Every project is a real thing built, run, and checked against a known result, then taught back to prove it holds. The same generator pipeline that ships my engineering repos ships these, one build at a time.

**What it isn't.** A claim of degrees I do not hold. Each track is honest: a route to command, not a credential. This is depth by choice across a few fields I love, not a race to collect all of them.

## 📊 Accountability

Progress is tracked out loud. The [build dashboard](https://roypiring.github.io/dashboard.html) is my accountability wall: what is onboarded, what is next, and how far each track has to go. Every project ships with a concrete self-check and a teach-back, so progress means proven, not read about.

## 🧭 How a project lands here

Each track carries a catalog of 100 ranked builds. I onboard them do-first, one standalone project at a time, through the same pipeline as the engineering repos: design-first, built by an orchestrated AI-agent swarm, validated against a known result, and shipped to its own repo with a plain-language guide. Safety and honesty are binding: public data, no medical or financial advice, and no claim past what the self-check proves.
